C++ is one of the most popular programming languages, especially in competitive programming. Currently, most young people prioritize learning C++ to build a foundation and programming mindset when first starting to explore programming. The basic C++ course is designed with the most basic and easy-to-understand knowledge to help you access it easily.

Course goal:

  • Helps you learn C++ from scratch quickly and easily.
  • Equips you with the necessary knowledge and skills to write simple C++ programs.
  • Helps you understand and use important concepts in C++.
  • Creates a foundation for you to self-study and develop more complex C++ programs.

Target audience:

  • This course is for beginners who have absolutely no programming knowledge, or those who have lost the basics and want to regain basic programming knowledge, specifically C++.

Teaching method:

  • The course combines theory and practice.
  • Students will learn through video lectures, theoretical readings, practice exercises, and theory tests.
  • Students will be able to ask and answer questions directly with their classmates and the course manager.

What will you learn?

  • After completing the course, students will have enough knowledge and skills to write simple C++ programs.
  • Students will understand and use important concepts in C++ easily.
  • Students will have a solid foundation to self-study and develop more complex C++ programs.
  • The course will help students improve their logical thinking, problem-solving, and programming skills effectively.
